STM32 QuadCoper Drone

My first project with STM32. I utilized the most commonly-used chip, STM32F103C8T6, which is enought to do data fusion of 6 axis angles. Double closed-loop PID control are implemented for self-stablizing purpose. Conversion between quaternions and Euler angles was achieved via in-built DMP library of MPU6050.

PCB Mini Challenge
